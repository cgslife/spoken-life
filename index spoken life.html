Before you begin
This app is a tool, not a treatment.
We care deeply about your whole wellbeing — not just your words. Please read this before continuing.
🤝 Spoken Life is a spiritual growth tool
This app helps you build daily habits around life-giving speech rooted in scripture. It is not a substitute for professional counseling, therapy, or medical care.
⛪ Community still matters
No app replaces your pastor, your faith community, or genuine human relationship. Use Spoken Life alongside those — not instead of them.
